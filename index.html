Frequently asked questions

What is Brightbase?
A design studio that helps builders ship faster. We combine brand strategy, design systems, and development into a single streamlined process.
How long does a project take?
Most projects ship in 2-4 weeks. We move fast because we use proven systems and AI-assisted workflows.
Do I own the code?
Yes, 100%. Everything we build is yours — the code, the design files, the brand assets. No lock-in.
Can I upgrade later?
Absolutely. All plans can be upgraded or downgraded at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.
